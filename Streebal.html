StreeBal - Women Strength

About StreeBal

Empowering women entrepreneurs through strategic business education, financial literacy, and mentorship.

Problems Faced

  • Few job opportunities due to gender roles
  • Limited access to financial education
  • Social and cultural barriers
  • Lack of business education

Our Solution

StreeBal provides a holistic skill development program, blending online and in-person learning with mentorship.

Program Highlights

  • 12-week structured curriculum
  • Hands-on workshops & self-paced online learning
  • One-on-one mentorship from industry experts
  • Networking sessions with successful entrepreneurs
